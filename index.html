Distributed-power Open Standards Alliance

DOSA preserves open interface standards for board-mounted DC-DC power modules, including package outlines, PCB footprints, pinouts, feature definitions, and reference specification drawings.

The archive supports Brick, Point-of-Load (POL), and Intermediate Bus Converter ecosystems where alternate sourcing and footprint compatibility matter.

Open and vendor neutral

Specifications are publicly available for broad industry adoption.

Interoperable by design

Common footprints, pinouts, and feature sets reduce design risk.

Engineer focused

CAD drawings, specifications, and reference materials first.

Standards Library

A public archive of DOSA standards for the power conversion engineering community. Domain: www.dosapower.org
